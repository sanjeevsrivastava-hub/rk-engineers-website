About R.K. Engineers Sales Limited

R.K. Engineers Sales Limited is one of the leading organizations providing complete water and wastewater management solutions.

Established in 1978, the company started its journey with pump spares and tube well accessories and gradually expanded into complete pumping solutions.

Today, R.K. Engineers provides KSB pump sales, service support, repair solutions, spare parts and technical assistance for industrial, municipal, commercial and residential applications.
